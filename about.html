Discover Our Story

Optima is a leading project planning company specialising in construction planning, project management, and providing online courses. With a wealth of industry expertise and a commitment to excellence, we offer comprehensive services and educational resources to clients worldwide. Our goal is to empower individuals and organisations with the knowledge and skills required for effective project planning and execution.

Here at Optima, we understand the unique challenges faced by construction projects. From large-scale commercial developments to residential renovations, our team of experienced professionals is equipped to handle projects of any size or complexity. We leverage cutting-edge software such as Primavera and PowerProject, alongside innovative methodologies, to optimise planning, scheduling, and resource allocation, helping our clients achieve their project goals within budget and on schedule.

Our Services

Optima provides expert project planning and construction planning services. Working closely with clients, we design meticulous project timelines, set milestones, and identify critical tasks. Our specialised expertise in utilising Primavera and PowerProject software enables us to create detailed construction plans, accurate cost estimates, and efficient resource allocation strategies.

Our dedicated project managers oversee every aspect of the project lifecycle. They effectively coordinate resources, monitor progress, manage risks, and facilitate communication among stakeholders to ensure smooth project execution. With a customer-centric approach, we prioritise client satisfaction and strive for excellence in every project we undertake.

At Optima, we believe that the key to success lies in a personalised approach. We take the time to understand our clients' unique needs, goals, and challenges, and tailor our project control services accordingly. Our team of experts works closely with you every step of the way, from initial planning to final analysis, to ensure that your project runs smoothly and efficiently. With a focus on excellence, attention to detail, and a can-do attitude, we are dedicated to helping you achieve your goals and exceed your expectations.
